## Overview

**Wisteria** is a narrative-driven experience that immerses players in a mystical world filled with wonder and discovery. The game emphasizes environmental storytelling and atmospheric exploration, creating an emotional journey through a beautifully crafted world.

### Key Features

- Rich narrative with multiple story threads
- Environmental storytelling techniques
- Atmospheric world design
- Emotional character moments
- Exploration-focused gameplay

### Narrative Design

The story of Wisteria was designed to be discovered through exploration and environmental clues rather than explicit exposition. Players piece together the narrative through careful observation and interaction with the world.

### Art Direction

The visual style emphasizes soft, natural colors and organic shapes, creating a dreamlike quality that supports the mystical themes of the narrative. Lighting plays a crucial role in setting the mood and guiding player attention.

### Design Goals

The primary goal was to create an experience that resonates emotionally with players while maintaining engagement through exploration and discovery. Every element of the game was designed to support the narrative and atmospheric goals.
