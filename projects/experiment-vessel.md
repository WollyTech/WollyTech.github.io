# Roles and Responsibilities
roles_responsibilities:
  level_design:
    - "Designing level mechanics and hazards"
    - "Designing level layout and enemy placement"
    - "Designing boss arenas"
  system_design:
    - "Designing movement, dashing and wall jumping mechanics."
    - "Using feedback from playtest sessions to tune movement and attacking fluidity"
  combat_design:
    - "Designing Rock-Paper-Scissors combat mechanic"
    - "Designing boss and enemy encounters and attack patterns"
    - "Creating audio and attack animations for every asset involved"
  ux_design:
    - "Designing HUD and Menus"
    - "Creating Tutorial for player to learn mechanics"

# Design Story
design_story:
  purpose_goal: '"Experiment Vessel" was created for the Breda University of Applied Science (BUAS) Entry assignment for the Design and Production track in the Creative Media and Game Technologies program. It was the first game I ever built and shipped.'
  concept:
    - "Inspired by the speed of combat in Katana Zero and Rock Paper Scissors to create an interesting gameplay loop"
  development:
    - "A speed run mode was thought developed but caused too many problems to implement properly that it had to be scrapped"
  post_development:
    - "Considering I am currently attending SETU and not BUAS, you'd think I didn't get in but I was accepted with an entry score of 82%"
