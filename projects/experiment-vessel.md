## Overview

**Experiment Vessel** is an experimental project that explores innovative vessel-based mechanics and player interaction. The game focuses on creating unique gameplay experiences through unconventional control schemes and physics-based interactions.

### Key Features

- Innovative vessel control mechanics
- Physics-based gameplay systems
- Exploratory level design
- Experimental interaction models

### Design Philosophy

This project served as a testing ground for experimental gameplay ideas. The goal was to push beyond traditional game mechanics and explore new ways of player interaction and control.

The development process emphasized rapid prototyping and iteration, allowing for quick testing of ideas and refinement based on playtesting feedback.

### Technical Challenges

Implementing the vessel-based mechanics required careful consideration of physics systems and control responsiveness. Balancing realism with fun gameplay was a constant challenge throughout development.
